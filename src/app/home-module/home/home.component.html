How It Works

Step 1: Generate QR Code

Create a unique QR code for your business using our simple generator. This QR code will be linked directly to your review page, making it easy for customers to provide feedback.

Step 2: Display QR Code

Place the QR code in prominent locations within your business premises. This could be on receipts, menus, business cards, or signage to ensure maximum visibility.

Step 3: Collect Reviews

Encourage customers to scan the QR code and leave their feedback. You can offer incentives like discounts or freebies for customers who complete a review.

Step 4: Analyze Feedback

Analyze the feedback to understand your customers' needs and improve services. Use the insights gained to make informed decisions that enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty.
